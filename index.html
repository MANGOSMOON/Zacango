Zoologico de Zacango

Information

Zoo featuring animal exhibits from birds to tigers, plus restaurants, a boating lake & a playground. The Zacango Ecological Park is a beautiful and spacious destination for family outings, located in Metepec, Mexico. With its wide variety of animal species, including primates, carnivores, birds, herbivores, farm animals, and even African species, the park has something for everyone.
